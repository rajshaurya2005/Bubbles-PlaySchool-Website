Our Story

Bubbles Playschool was founded with a simple yet powerful vision — to create a space where young children feel loved, excited, and inspired to learn. What started as a small classroom with big dreams has grown into a vibrant learning community that families trust and children adore.

Our founders, passionate educators and parents themselves, envisioned a school that goes beyond academics. A place where children discover the joy of learning through play, where creativity is celebrated, and where every child is seen, heard, and valued. Today, Bubbles stands as a testament to that vision — a colourful, caring home away from home.

Over the years, we have nurtured hundreds of young minds through our Playgroup, Nursery, PP1, and PP2 programs, building a strong foundation for their journey ahead.

Mission & Vision

What drives us every day at Bubbles Playschool.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where every child can develop holistically — emotionally, socially, cognitively, and physically — through play-based learning and age-appropriate curriculum.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and loved preschool in our community, recognized for inspiring a lifelong love of learning, fostering creativity, and empowering every child to reach their full potential.

Meet Our Director

Mrs. Priya Sharma

Founder & Director

With over 15 years of experience in early childhood education, Mrs. Priya Sharma founded Bubbles Playschool with the belief that every child deserves a joyful and nurturing start. Her approach blends modern teaching methodologies with traditional values, creating a unique learning environment that parents and children love.

Our Values

The pillars that guide everything we do at Bubbles.

Care

Every child is treated with love, patience, and respect. We create bonds that make children feel safe and valued.

Creativity

We encourage imagination and self-expression through art, music, story-telling, and free play.

Curiosity

We nurture natural curiosity by providing hands-on experiences and encouraging questions and exploration.

Community

We build a warm school community where parents, teachers, and children grow together as a family.

Joy

We believe learning should be fun! Every day at Bubbles is filled with laughter, play, and wonderful memories.
